About Chevron Corporation

Chevron Corporation is one of the world's largest integrated energy companies, incorporated in 1879 and headquartered in San Ramon, California. The company participates in every facet of the oil, natural gas, and geothermal energy industries — from exploration and production to refining, marketing, and transportation.

Chevron holds major upstream positions across the Permian Basin, Gulf of Mexico, Kazakhstan (Tengizchevroil), and Australia (Gorgon and Wheatstone LNG). Its 2023 acquisition of PDC Energy added DJ Basin and Permian acreage. The pending $53bn all-stock Hess merger extends Chevron into the prolific Stabroek Block in Guyana.

Downstream operations include refineries at Richmond, El Segundo, and Pascagoula, together with a global retail network under the Chevron, Texaco, and Caltex brands. Chevron is a quarterly dividend payer every year since 1912.
